M.S. Dhoni

The Living Legend of Indian Cricket

Here's a time line of MS. Dhoni's life:

  • 1981 - Born in Ranchi, India
  • 1999-2000 - Played Ranji Trophy by representing Bihar.
  • 2001-2003 - Travelling Ticket Examiner at Kaharagpur Railway Station
  • 2004 - Made ODI debut against Bangladesh
  • 2005 - Played first test match against Sri Lanka. Beacme Wicket Keeper of India
  • 2007 - Became captain of the Indian National Cricket Team. Led India to the historic T20 World cup win.
  • 2008 and 2009 - Won the ICC Player of the Year Award
  • 2008 - Joins Chennai Super Kings, most successful team of Indian Premier League
  • 2010 - Married Sakshi Singh Rawat.
  • 2011 - Led India to win their second World Cup Title after 28 years
  • 2014 - Retires from Test Cricket
  • 2015 - His daughter Ziva was Born.
  • 2016 - Retired from captaincy.
  • 2016 - Biopic M.S Dhoni - The Untold Story made on his life
  • 2018 - Received Padma Bhushan, Third Highest Civilian honour in India
  • 2018 - Chennai Super Kings won their third IPL title under Leadership of MS. Dhoni.
  • 2019 - Represented India in the World Cup held in England.
